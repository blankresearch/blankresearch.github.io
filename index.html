Abstract Abstract Abstract
Blank Research is a founding engineering lab. We help founders, startups, and established companies create new products and enter new markets by turning their visions into robust platforms.
Our methodology is a hybrid of market research and blitzkrieg development (fast execution), producing an MVP or prototype that fits the market and is agile enough for remodeling and adaptation.

Our principal work can be summarized as follows:
● Shape the vision into actionable features
● Research the market to validate demand
● Design, Code, test, and deploy the product
● Define a monetization strategy
● Launch and grow an early adopter community
● Track budget, costs, and burn rate
● Help recruit a core team
● Iterate until product maturity

We work closely with founders and early teams, and we only take 1 or 2 projects at a time, which usually lasts from 3 to 6 months. Quality and timing are important to us, but also is organization, communication, and fast execution. As these are the foundations that make or break great companies, and we don't compromise on neither of them.
